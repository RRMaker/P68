Campus Life

school room

Was built in 2019

This is the entrance room of the school. It seems regular, but it may be one of the coolest rooms. The pandemic set lots of people back to virtual learning, so now when those students come to school, the first thing they see wil be the entrance room, amazing them.

school track

Was built in 2019

This is the track for the school. It plays a big role because every grade uses these. 6th grade uses it to do mile runs. 7th grade uses it for track course. And 8th grade uses to train.

school theatre

Was built in 2019

This is the school theatre. The people who take the theatre elective usually work in this room. They mainly film movies and make plays. There is also Tech theatre where they use tech to edit and polish up the movie.

Whats New

  • More F2f people increasing campus population

    In Lawler Middle School the campus is going to go back to normal starting August 12, 2021. Because of the pandemic, the campus took a great hit, decreasing to only 300 people, much less than the usual 1000. But now since the COVID vaccines have been distributed, the students are more comfortable going.I know this will impact greatly for the better.

  • Option for virtual school has been disabled

    In the earlier passage I mentioned that more people are returning to F2F. However,there are a cluster of people that still chose to go virtual. At first the option was open, but for it to stay open, it had to become a bill for the government, which needed to be approved. The bill was approved, but there was no final approval. Because of the absence of the final approval, they had no choice but to disable the option until final approval was given. But there is still hope for it, because if the bill gets the approval, then the option can come back.
